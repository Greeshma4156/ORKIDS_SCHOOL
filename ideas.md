# Orkids High School Website — Future Improvements

This document tracks design and content ideas to refine the Orkids website.

---

## 📢 Announcement Bar
* **Concept:** Move the "ADMISSIONS OPEN 2026-2027" badge out of the hero column and turn it into a top-level announcement bar.
* **Location:** Spanned 100% width, placed above the navigation header.
* **Style:** Warm gold background (`--secondary-color`) with dark navy text.

## 🖼️ Hero Image Refinement
* **Concept:** Replace the dense "Toppers Banner" in the Hero section with a warm, welcoming, uncluttered photo of the school campus or students.
* **Alternative Location for Banner:** Move the congratulations banner down to the "SSC Board Toppers" section as a downloadable image link or display it inside the welcome pop-up modal.

## 📅 Notice Board & Notice Feed
* **Concept:** Add a small "Notice Board" column/widget next to the Principal's message or right above the admission timeline.
* **Content:** Upcoming parent-teacher meetings, exam timetables, or holiday announcements.

## 📚 Curriculum Detail
* **Concept:** Detail the school hours and curriculum focus.
* **Content:** 
  * SSC board syllabus approach
  * Computer lab courses
  * Extracurricular activities (Sports, Arts)
  * School hours: Mon-Fri (8:30 AM - 3:30 PM), Sat (8:30 AM - 12:30 PM)

## 📷 Photo Gallery
* **Concept:** A clean grid layout with photos of sports day, festivals, and science fairs to show life at Orkids.
