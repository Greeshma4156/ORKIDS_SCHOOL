Why "Orkids"?

Our name is a promise. Formed from Our + Kids, Orkids High School was built on the simple belief that every student who walks through our doors is nurtured, protected, and taught just like our very own child.

Individual Attention

Small class sizes ensuring every student receives personalized guidance and support.

Strong Foundation

Emphasis on core subjects and character building from the early stages of learning.

Safe Environment

CCTV-monitored campus with trained staff ensuring the highest levels of student safety.

Holistic Development

Balanced focus on sports, arts, and music alongside rigorous academic programs.
